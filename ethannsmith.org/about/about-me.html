    About Me ✏️

    Student | Creator | Goaltender

    Ethan Smith

    Passionate Hockey Goaltender & Aspiring Sports Medicine Student

    Howdy! I am Ethan Smith, a dedicated goaltender in hockey and a committed student. I attend Valley Catholic High School in Beaverton, Oregon, where I’ve spent the past 10 years enjoying both academics and athletics.

    Currently, I play for the Vancouver Rangers hockey team in Vancouver, Washington. With a strong work ethic both on the ice and in the classroom, I’ve cultivated a deep love for Physical Education, Science, and STEM-related subjects.

    I’m proficient in 3D design, creation, and coding (HTML, CSS, and JS). My goal in college is to pursue a degree in Sports Medicine while continuing my hockey career by playing for a college team.
